## How to Use This Document

1. **Regular Updates:** Add lessons as soon as they're learned, while details are fresh
2. **Be Honest:** Document failures openly - they're the most valuable lessons
3. **Include Metrics:** Quantify improvements/impacts where possible
4. **Share Widely:** These lessons benefit the entire team and future projects
5. **Review Periodically:** Revisit lessons to see if they still apply

## Retrospective Questions

Use these questions in retrospectives to identify new lessons:
1. What surprised us this sprint/release?
2. What took longer than expected? Why?
3. What went smoother than expected? Why?
4. What would we do differently if starting over?
5. What knowledge would have helped us earlier?
6. What assumptions proved wrong?
7. What patterns are we seeing repeatedly?
