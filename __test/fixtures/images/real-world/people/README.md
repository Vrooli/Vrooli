## Testing Considerations

These images were selected to provide:
- **Demographic diversity**: Different ethnicities, ages, and genders
- **Age range coverage**: From infant to adult
- **Group size variation**: Single person to crowd
- **Different lighting conditions**: Indoor and outdoor settings
- **Various poses**: Frontal faces, profiles, and candid shots

## Ethical Note

These images are from Unsplash and are free to use. When using for testing:
- Respect privacy and ethical considerations
- Use only for testing purposes
- Do not use for training discriminatory models
- Consider GDPR and privacy regulations in your testing
